Burgers-Recipe

Description

Follow this recipe for delicious burgers.

Ingredients

  • Beef Patty from Sams
  • Big Sliced Tomatoes
  • Square Cheese
  • Onions
  • Dill Pickles
  • Mushrooms
  • Iceberg Lettuce
  • Chipotle Mayonnaise
  • Burger Buns

Steps

  1. Grill patties til they not bloody no more
  2. Cook mushrooms
  3. Spread Mayo all over your buns
  4. Lay lettuce down on bottom bun
  5. Stack tomatoes on lettuce
  6. Stack patty on tomatoes
  7. Stack square cheese on patty
  8. Onions
  9. Put the pickles on top
  10. Lay the top bun on top the stack
