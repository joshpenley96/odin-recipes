Oatmeal-Recipe

Description

Follow this recipe for delicious Oatmeal.

Ingredients

  • Steel cut oats
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • Raisins
  • Blueberries

Steps

  1. Pour some oats in a bowl about 4 liters
  2. Pour some milk in there too maybe a few gallons
  3. Brown sugar too, 2 pounds
  4. Mix it all together
  5. Throw in microwave and cook til it boils over
  6. Stirr and add a stick of butter
  7. Throw some blueberries and raisins in there
  8. Enjoy! (That means that was the last step)
